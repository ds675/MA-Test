[chat] position:sticky on any element inside `#header-group` won't stick across page scroll — the element's containing block is limited to #header-group's own height (which equals its content), so sticky has no room to "stick". Target `#header-group` itself via `body:has(.custom-header--sticky) #header-group { position: sticky; top: 0; }` since it's a direct child of body and therefore its containing block is the viewport.
